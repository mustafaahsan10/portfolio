## Problem

I wanted to ship a polished, production-shaped AI product — not a demo — with the
real-world concerns most prototypes skip: auth, streaming UX, and cost control.

## Approach

Built on the Next.js App Router with server-side streaming of model output to a
React client, authenticated sessions, and per-user rate limiting backed by
Postgres. The UI handles partial responses, retries, and error states cleanly.

## Outcome

A production-ready template for AI features: fast streaming UX, secure auth, and
rate limits that keep model costs predictable.
